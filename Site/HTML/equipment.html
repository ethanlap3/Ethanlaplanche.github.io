This page contains all of the equipment the players need to be able to play in the olympic hockey team.
Equipment
Name Use Who?
Kicker A protective device worn by a goalkeeper and fastened over the front and sides of the shoe to allow him or her to kick the ball. Goalkeeper
Stick A hook-shaped device made from wood and/or composite materials) with a flat face on the left side for hitting the ball. All players
Helmet Used to protect head and face. Goalkeeper
Elbow pads Used to protect the goalkeeper's body. Goalkeeper
Leg pads Used to protect the goalkeeper's body. Goalkeeper
Shin pads Used to protect shins. All players
Shoes Special shoes are worn to give good grip for running and turning. All players
Ball Made of hard plastic, usually white and with a circumference of approximately 230mm.
